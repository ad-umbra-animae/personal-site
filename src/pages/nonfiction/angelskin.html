--- layout: layouts/post.njk title: Angel's Skin is Repulsive date: 2025-01-03T12:00:00-04:00 tags: [poetry, life, fridge magnets] collectionName: nonfiction category: Non-Fiction ---

Men do blow after
A hot black fish-woman
Drools pink blood.

Angel skin is repulsive
When the heart of glass
Gives you no joy.

Put me by red summer
Only to sit like a fool
Bleed open, ugly, and hot
Heave but still shine wild
